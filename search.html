HELPFUL BITS: When searching with a product number, it is sometimes more productive to use only a part of the number. Usually, the first and last few characters have to do with either the manufacturer or the product finish or size. The core id # is usually universal. For example, the product number 647-P02099-SS-A could be easily found using P02099-SS or even 2099. Sometimes you will get other results that closely match the stripped down product number.
