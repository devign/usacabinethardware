1) How do I order samples?
Samples are ordered just like any other item on our site, simply add any item you want to sample to your shopping cart and proceed to checkout. For more information, see our policies page.

2) Will I get charged a restocking fee when I return the samples?
Unfortunately, yes. Please see our policy on samples.

3) I have these hinges that have broken and they have these numbers stamped on them, can you tell me what they are?
Probably not. The only way we can even attempt to identify what kind or brand of hinge you have is if you send us some pictures. If know that you have Blum or Amerock hinges, please contact them directly. Click here to go to our Blum section for information on how to contact them.

4) How do I tell if I have overlay or inset hinges/How do I measure the overlay/inset? Please visit our Cabinet Hinge Terms and Cabinet Hinge Styles pages for information on the different types of hinges and how to measure them.
