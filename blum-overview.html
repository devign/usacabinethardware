CONCEALED HINGE PROGRAM
Panel Cabinet Hinges

The CLIP and INSERTA series are specifically designed to offer makers of European style cabinets (or frameless) the advantages of concealed hinges , plus much more. Both series feature three-way independent adjustment and self-closing action and are available in various opening degrees and applications.

CLIP hinges have become the standard for fast and efficient cabinet construction and installation. Tool-free mounting of the hinge to the mounting plate means installers can quickly remove the doors to make the cabinet lighter and reinstall them without affecting the independent adjustments.

Blum's INSERTA hinges offer all of the benefits of the Clip hinge plus the easiest tool-free installation method of hinge to pre-bored door in the industry. With just fingertip pressure, the cover is pushed closed to expand both gripper arms in the center hole for a firm connection.

The MODUL hinge has been discontinued by Blum.

Blum's ANGLED hinge program covers applications between +46° and -45° in increments of 5°. Permits doors to be hinged to angled front cabinets in five different corner styles from maximum overlay to inset and mitered corner.
Face Frame Application With Panel Cabinet Hinges
These mounting plate profiles make it possible to use panel cabinet hinges on face frame cabinets.
Frame Cabinet Hinges
COMPACT 33
This series is specifically designed to offer makers of the frame cabinets the advantages of concealed hinges. Various door overlays are achieved by slide-on mounting of COMPACT 33 series 100° hinges to one of a variety of mounting plates.
COMPACT 33 hinges are designed to slide-on to edge mount, wraparound or face applied mounting plates. The cup boring pattern is the same as for the CLIP and MODUL series. Hinges and mounting plates are nickel-plated die cast.


Side Adjustment
Loosen screw on hinge arm and adjust door to desired position; tighten screw.

Height Adjustment
Loosen screw(s) on all mounting plates, adjust door and retighten screws.
COMPACT 38
New one-piece Face Frame hinge for 1/4", 1/2" and 3/4" overlay doors. One screw height adjustment and one screw side to side adjustment. Stamped steel. 105° opening.
